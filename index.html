About Us

The Turkish Music and Arts Society of Florida is devoted to keeping the spirit of Turkish culture alive and sharing it with the world through the universal language of music and the arts. Our mission is to nurture a vibrant community where traditions are honored, new generations find inspiration, and cultural bonds grow stronger. Through concerts, cultural gatherings, and educational programs, we take pride in presenting the richness of Turkish music, art, and history to both our community and the wider public.

Turkuaz Choir

At the heart of this mission shines the Turkuaz Choir, the vocal ensemble of our society, under the direction of our music director and conductor Can Yıldırım. Bringing together gifted voices united by a passion for Turkish choral and folk music, the choir performs across Florida, touching hearts and building bridges through the beauty of song. Each performance is not only a celebration of heritage but also an invitation for audiences of all backgrounds to experience the charm and depth of Turkish music.

To learn more about the choir, explore upcoming performances, or become a part of our journey, please visit the Turkuaz Choir page.
