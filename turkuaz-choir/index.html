Turkuaz Choir

The Turkuaz Choir is the vocal ensemble of the Turkish Music and Arts Society of Florida, directed by Can Yıldırım. Our choir brings together talented voices passionate about Turkish choral and folk music, performing across Florida and sharing the beauty of Turkish music with diverse audiences.

Each performance is a celebration of heritage and an invitation for everyone to experience the charm and depth of Turkish music. Join us for upcoming concerts, learn more about our members, or get involved!
